Instructions:
To the left is the canvas, beneath it are a series of controls that can be used to control the behaviour of the render.
Beneath this card are toggles for lighting types. Directional light comes in at a fixed direction, point light emits from the swinging light bulb. If both light types are turned off, lighting will be disabled and the scene will be rendered using raw color values.
